Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What areas do you cover?
We provide nationwide coverage across the UK. Based in the West Midlands, we regularly serve Birmingham, Manchester, Liverpool, London, Leeds, and all major cities. We're happy to travel anywhere in the UK for your project.
Do you offer free quotes?
Yes! We offer completely free site surveys and quotations. Our team will visit your premises, assess your requirements, and provide you with a detailed, no-obligation quote on the spot.
How long does installation take?
Installation time varies depending on the project size and complexity. Most standard shop front installations take 1-3 days. We pride ourselves on quick turnaround times and will provide you with a realistic timeline during the quote process.
Do you provide emergency repair services?
Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency repair and replacement services. If your shutters or shop front are damaged, call us immediately at +44 7471 043827 and we'll get someone out to you as quickly as possible.
What types of shutters do you install?
We install a wide range of shutters including roller shutters, steel security grills, aluminium shutters, fire-rated shutters, and perforated shutters. All our products are manufactured to the highest standards and meet UK building regulations.
Are your installations guaranteed?
Absolutely. All our installations come with comprehensive guarantees. We use only high-quality materials and our experienced team ensures every installation meets the highest standards of workmanship.
Can you match or beat competitor prices?
Yes! We offer a price matching service. If you have a like-for-like quote from a competitor, we'll do our best to match or beat it while maintaining our high standards of quality and service.
